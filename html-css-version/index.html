Our Story

Founded in 2010, ModernHome began as a small workshop crafting custom furniture. Today, we've grown into a design destination offering carefully curated home goods that blend functionality with timeless aesthetics.

Every piece in our collection is selected with intention, focusing on quality materials, artisanal craftsmanship, and designs that stand the test of time.
